Chocolate Cake

By Ola Nordmann

01.06.2025

This rich and moist chocolate cake is perfect for celebrations or an indulgent treat. It's easy to make and incredibly delicious!

Ingredients:

  • 200g all-purpose flour
  • 200g sugar
  • 100g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
